BEBEDEPINO story

KATIE and RYAN have the same philosophy of beautiful life. Bebe De Pino’s story began in 2010.

After their first child, Sol was born, clothes and accessories that had made for her, were introduced to the neighbors, and the brand was launched naturally. Bebe De Pino is a Spanish word for “baby pine”, inspired by the name of their child, SOL. KATIE who inspired by the children’s world, RYAN who loves the enthusiastic colors were made impressive collections, and prove their talent. By opening about 100stores, Bebe De Pino has been loving from customers and it’s grown to become one of Korea’s leading brands.

In 2016, LACOSTE Chief Designer Ally joins as creative director, she expands the brand's artistic value. ALLY loves heritage and has an excellent ability to reinterpret the retro modernly.
